Who We Are

Transforming spaces into sustainable, beautiful environments that inspire and endure.

Our Story

Innovative EnviroSH is a Ghanaian-based enterprise committed to enhancing both natural and occupational environments. We specialize in delivering quality innovative landscaping services alongside comprehensive occupational health and safety solutions especially in the areas of ergonomics and driving safety. Establised with a vision to enhance innovative indoor and outdoor environments and workplace safety, InnoEnviroSH combines creativity, technical expertise, and regulatory knowledge to deliver standard results to residential, commercial, and industrial clients

Today, we're proud to be a leading force in innovative landscaping solutions, serving both residential and commercial clients across the region. Our team has grown to include certified horticulturists, landscape architects, and skilled craftsmen, all united by our passion for creating extraordinary outdoor spaces.

Our Vision

To be a household name in the provision of landscaping and occupational health and safety (OHS), especially in the fields of ergonomics and driving safety, with a strong commitment to client satisfaction.

Our Mission

To create sustainable, aesthetically pleasing indoor and outdoor spaces while promoting a culture of well-being in workplaces across industries.
